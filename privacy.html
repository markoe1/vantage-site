VIRALSIG — Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Elegant Solar Inc ("VIRALSIG," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, discloses, and protects information in connection with the VIRALSIG service (the "Service"). VIRALSIG helps creators generate, manage, and publish short-form video clips from their long-form content and publish them to the creator's own connected platform accounts, such as TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, X, and Facebook. By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Elegant Solar Inc — 10206 E Nichols Ave, Mesa, AZ 85209, United States

1. Information We Collect

  • Account & contact information you provide, such as your email address, when you contact us or use the Service.
  • Connected-platform information. When you connect a platform account (such as TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, X, or Facebook), we access only the data covered by the permissions you grant. For TikTok, connected via Login Kit, this includes:
    • user.info.basic — your basic TikTok profile (such as open_id and display name) to confirm the correct account is connected.
    • video.publish — permission to publish video clips to your own TikTok profile via the TikTok Content Posting API, at your direction.
  • Content you provide for processing, such as the videos and clips you choose to generate, manage, or publish.
  • Authentication tokens issued by connected platforms, used to perform the actions you authorize.
  • Technical and usage data, such as basic log information needed to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the Service.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide the Service — generating, managing, and publishing clips to your connected accounts at your direction.
  • To authenticate you and maintain your connected-account authorization.
  • To operate, secure, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To respond to your support requests and communicate with you.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only:

  • With connected platforms (such as TikTok) as necessary to perform the actions you authorize.
  • With service providers who process data on our behalf (for example, hosting and infrastructure), under confidentiality obligations.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users or the Service.

4. Data Storage & Security

We retain information only as long as needed to provide the Service and for legitimate business or legal purposes. Authentication tokens are stored securely and used only to perform the actions you authorize. We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, including encryption of sensitive credentials, to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

5. Data Retention

We keep connected-account tokens and related data only while your account connection is active or as needed to provide the Service. When you disconnect your account, revoke access, or request deletion, we delete or de-identify the associated data within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Revoke access to any connected account at any time in that platform's settings (for TikTok, under Security & Permissions); you may also disconnect within the Service.
  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information by contacting us at support@viralsig.com.
  • Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA. We honor applicable rights and do not discriminate against you for exercising them.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. International Users

We operate from the United States. If you access the Service from outside the United States, you understand your information may be processed and stored in the United States, where data-protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be indicated as appropriate.
