Privacy Policy - VIRALSIG

Last updated: 2026-06-20

VIRALSIG, operated by Elegant Solar Inc, explains how it handles data when integrating with TikTok.

Information We Access

When you authorize the app via TikTok Login Kit, we access only the permissions you grant:

  • user.info.basic - your basic TikTok profile (open_id, display name) to confirm the correct account is connected.
  • video.publish - permission to publish your own video clips to your TikTok profile via the Content Posting API.

How We Use Information

To publish video clips to your TikTok account at your direction. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Data Retention & Revocation

Access tokens are stored only as needed to perform posting and may be revoked anytime in your TikTok settings under Security & Permissions.
