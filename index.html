VIRALSIG

VIRALSIG, operated by Elegant Solar Inc, turns a creator's long-form videos into short clips and publishes them to the creator's own TikTok profile via the TikTok Content Posting API. The creator authorizes the app to post on their behalf.

Integration: Login Kit (user.info.basic) and Content Posting API (video.publish).
