How it works

A simple, creator-controlled workflow. You stay in charge of what gets published.

1

Connect your accounts

Authorize VIRALSIG for the platforms you use — TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, or X. We only receive the permissions you explicitly grant.

2

Generate clips

VIRALSIG analyzes your long-form video and produces short, engaging clip candidates with captions.

3

Review & approve

You review the generated clips. Nothing is published without passing our brand-safety checks.

4

Publish everywhere

Approved clips are posted to your own connected accounts across TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and X.

What VIRALSIG does

An end-to-end pipeline for repurposing long-form video into short-form content.

Moment detection

Automatically finds the most engaging moments in your long-form videos as clip candidates.

Variant generation

Creates multiple clip and caption variations so you can publish the strongest version.

Brand-safety gate

Every clip passes automated safety checks before it can be queued for publishing.

Multi-platform publishing

Post approved clips to your own connected accounts on TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and X — with more platforms planned as future integrations.

Account connection

Securely connect each platform with granular, revocable permissions — TikTok uses official Login Kit.

You stay in control

You decide what publishes, and you can revoke access at any time from each platform's settings.
